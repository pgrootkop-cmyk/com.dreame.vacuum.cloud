Give your Dreame robot vacuum a place in your smart home. Start, stop and dock your vacuum from Homey, clean specific rooms or zones, send it to any spot on the map, and keep an eye on the battery, consumables and dock. A live map widget on your dashboard shows exactly where your robot is while it works.

Build powerful automations with flow cards for every part of the cleaning routine: vacuum the kitchen after dinner, mop when everyone has left the house, get a notification when the dust bag is full, or switch on the lights in the room your robot is currently cleaning. Works with your Dreame Home account and supports multiple vacuums and multi-floor homes.
